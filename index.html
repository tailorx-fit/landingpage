Home/Process

Three steps
between a phone
and a perfect fit.

No tape measure. No second fitting. No guesswork. A guided scan, a touch of artificial intelligence, and tailor-ready numbers delivered in seconds.

01 Capture

Stand. Rotate. Scanned.

Our mobile guide walks you through a 360° rotation — adjusting lighting, framing, and distance in real time. The scan takes under a minute, and any smartphone with a camera works.

✓ Real-time pose feedback: "step back," "adjust lighting"
✓ Works on iOS, Android, and web browsers
✓ Accepts 360° video or multi-angle photos
✓ Built-in countdown timer and posture prompts
02 Compute

AI vision reads the body.

Pose estimation and 3D reconstruction models extract twelve key measurement points — chest, waist, hips, shoulders, sleeve, inseam, and more — with ninety-five percent accuracy against manual tape measures.

✓ MediaPipe + custom CNN pose estimation
✓ 3D mesh reconstruction pipeline
✓ Sub-10 second processing time
✓ Confidence scoring for each measurement
03 Deliver

Tailor-ready numbers.

Measurements arrive instantly in your dashboard — exportable to PDF, CSV, or fed straight into your pattern software. Send a measurement request link to any client, track scan completion, and set reminders.

✓ PDF and CSV export for pattern software
✓ Send measurement request links to clients
✓ Track scan status and send reminders
✓ Interactive 3D body model in dashboard
